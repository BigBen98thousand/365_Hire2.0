Terms & Conditions

1. ELIGIBILITY
Renters must be at least 19 years of age (age may vary by car).
Proof of valid driver’s license, and a DVLA check code.

2. RENTAL PERIOD
The rental period begins when the vehicle is picked up and ends when it is returned.
Late returns may incur additional charges.

3. PAYMENT & DEPOSIT
Fees must be paid at the time of pickup or as specified. We accept cash and bank transfer only.

4. INSURANCE
All our vehicles come with fully comprehensive insurance.

5. FUEL POLICY
Vehicles are provided with a full tank of gas, requiring return with a full tank; failure to do so will lead to additional charges, plus an admin fee.

6. MILEAGE
All our cars include a mileage acceptance of 200 miles per day, 1500 miles per week, and 7000 miles per month.

7. VEHICLE USE
The vehicle can only be driven by authorized drivers listed in the rental agreement.

8. MAINTENANCE & REPAIRS
Please report any mechanical issues promptly.
Unauthorized repairs or modifications may lead to additional charges.

9. ACCIDENTS & DAMAGE
Any damages or accidents to the vehicle should be reported directly to us.
Responsibility for damages may vary based on the rental agreement and insurance.

10. CANCELLATION POLICY
Free Cancellation Period: Customers can cancel their booking up to 48 hours before the scheduled pickup time without any charges.
Cancellation Fees: A fee of £50.00 will be applied to cancellations made less than 48 hours before pickup. Cancellations on the day will result in a 100% fee of the first day's rental cost.
